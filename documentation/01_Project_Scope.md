# PROJECT SCOPE
<br>

## Project Title

Vovers Business & Finance Analytics Platform

<br>

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to design and build a complete end-to-end Business & Finance Analytics solution for a fictional omnichannel consumer electronics retail company named **Vovers**.

The project aims to simulate how a Business & Finance Analyst works in a real organization by covering the complete analytics lifecycle—from database design and data generation to business analysis, financial modelling, dashboard development, and executive reporting.

<br>

## Business Background

Vovers is a fictional mid-sized consumer electronics retailer headquartered in Gurugram, India. The company operates through both physical retail stores and online sales channels across multiple cities.

As the business expands, management requires a centralized analytics platform that enables data-driven decision making across sales, finance, inventory, customer behaviour, procurement, and operations.

This project is designed to fulfil those business requirements.
