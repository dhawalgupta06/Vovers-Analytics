# Data Generation Rules - Products Table

## Purpose

This document defines the product catalog for the Vovers ERP project.

It specifies the product categories, brands, and product models that will be included in the synthetic dataset. The document serves as the foundation for generating records in the **Products** and **Product_Variants** tables while ensuring a realistic and consistent product assortment throughout the project.

The catalog has been designed to represent a mid-sized omnichannel consumer electronics retailer operating in India. Products are selected based on current market relevance, practical retail availability, and business demand to support realistic purchasing, inventory management, sales transactions, and analytical reporting.
