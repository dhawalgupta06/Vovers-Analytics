# Data Generation Rules - Suppliers Table

## Purpose

This document defines the business rules, assumptions, and distributions used to generate realistic synthetic data for the **Suppliers** table of the **Vovers ERP Database**.

The objective is to ensure that the generated supplier dataset reflects a realistic procurement network for a mid-sized Indian consumer electronics retailer, making it suitable for:

- SQL Analysis
- Python Analytics
- Excel Dashboards
- Power BI Reports
- Business Case Studies
- Portfolio Projects

## Business Assumptions

- Suppliers are concentrated in India's major electronics manufacturing and distribution hubs.
- Supplier location is independent of store location.
- Multiple suppliers may operate from the same city.
- A supplier may supply products belonging to one or more categories.
- Supplier records are retained permanently for historical procurement analysis.
- All suppliers are assumed to be active throughout the project timeline.
